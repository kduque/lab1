Triton Wash
Prototype Engineer

In a team of 5, we designed a mobile app to improve an on-campus student's experience when using the UCSD laundry facilites.

After many interviews, and paper prototypes, we tackled these problems: how many and which machines are available, how much time does my machine have left, and paying for the service through the app

The actual iOS prototype is still being implemented but the design itself placed at the UXSD Design Competition Spring 2014.
